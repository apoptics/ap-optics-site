About Me

Avnish Pankhania BSc. Optometry, MCOptom, Prof. Cert. Medical Retina – GOC Registered Optometrist

I’m a warm, patient-focused locum optometrist with a passion for delivering high-quality eye care in both independent and multiple practice settings. With strong clinical knowledge and a friendly, professional approach, I strive to provide a seamless experience for both patients and practices. Whether it’s short-notice cover or regular bookings, I bring reliability, adaptability, and a people-first ethos to every clinic I join.

I am proficient in a wide range of optical systems, including Socrates, Acuitas, and Optix, allowing me to hit the ground running in any practice environment. I am also WOPEC accredited in Cataract and Glaucoma, MECS accredited, and have a particular interest in Myopia Management and OCT interpretation.

Myopia Management & OCT

Myopia control is a growing concern for both parents and practitioners. I have extensive experience in the latest myopia management strategies, including specialized contact lenses and lifestyle guidance for long-term visual health.

I am also proficient in interpreting OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) scans, allowing for early detection and management of retinal and optic nerve conditions. These skills enhance my ability to deliver advanced and proactive eye care.
